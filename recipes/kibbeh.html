Kibbeh


Description:


One of my favorite dishes from my childhood. My family is Syrian
and we ate kibbeh all the time. A wonderful, savory dish that was
always a favorite for parties or celebrations in our church and
home.


Ingredients:


  • Bulger wheat
  • Mint leaves
  • Onion
  • Cumin
  • Allspice
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lean ground lamb
  • Olive Oil

Steps:


  1. Prep the things
  2. Roast the meaty bits
  3. Add veggies and spices to the meaty bits
  4. Poor broth and paste
  5. Roast in oven
  6. Plate the things
